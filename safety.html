Elite's Safety Standard

To better serve our community

At Elite, we prioritize the safety of the partners and the customers on our platform. In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, we are rolling out a number of efforts to help foster a safe environment to better serve our communities.

FACE COVERINGS DURING BOOKINGS

We are supplying partners with face coverings to wear while completing bookings as per the CDC’s recommendation to slow the spread of the virus.

DAILY SELF-CERTIFICATIONS FOR PARTNERS

We are requiring every partners to confirm that they are not experiencing a fever, cough, or shortness of breath and committing to following CDC and local health regulations on a daily basis.

TOOLS TO ENABLE A SAFE ENVIRONMENT

We are communicating the CDC’s health and safety guidelines and best practices to customers and partners to be followed before, during and after a booking.

HIGH-GRADE CLEANING SUPPLIES

We are providing partners with guidance on using EPA-approved cleaning products.

Supporting our community

1 TO 1 DONATIONS TO HOSPITALS

For every set of masks and gloves we provide to a partners, we are also donating a set to local hospitals in need of these supplies.

FINANCIAL ASSISANCE

We are providing financial assistance to partners who have tested positive for COVID-19.

EARNING OPPORTUNITIES

We are connecting partners with opportunities to continue to earn money by providing essential services to you and to your community.
